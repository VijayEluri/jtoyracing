jtoyracing

Overview

JToyRacing is a game demo that intends to use toys as vehicles to compete in different parts of a house. Although it doesn't have objectives at the moment, it is playable. There is one environment where the race takes place and one vehicle available. There are two checkpoints in the race track that restore the vehicle's health:
